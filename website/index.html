About

The idea behind GroceryAssistant

What is GroceryAssistant

GroceryAssistant is your personal mobile tool, that can improve your grocery experience. The main feature is that, it provides the users with a personal grocery list. Also, GroceryAssistant helps users to navigate through the store by letting the users know where the items are located in the store.

We also integrate Google Maps so that you can look for grocery stores near you. GroceryAssistant can also tell you the detailed informations about the stores. For instance, the opening hours, address, and website.

On top of that, it also has other features such as providing you with nutrition facts and suggested recipes.

Who are the intended users

It is primarily for household grocery shoppers. But, it's for anyone, really! Whether you do your groceries often or not, we think GroceryAssistant surely helps anyone who want to do their groceries.

Features

Our goals is to enhance your grocery experience. This is the overview of features we offer!

Department Look-up
